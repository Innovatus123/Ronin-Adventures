Destination Overview

The Kingdom
of Gold.

Thailand has been a Kingdom continuously since 1238 CE — the Sukhothai Dynasty founding what became the longest continuous monarchy in Southeast Asia. The evidence is everywhere: 40,000 Buddhist temples, a royal cuisine that requires 9 separate types of basil, and a ceremonial calendar that the country literally organizes its year around.

Ronin Adventures designs Thailand itineraries around the country's three distinct geographies: the ancient temple circuit in the north, the hill tribe and jungle access from Chiang Mai, and the limestone island clusters of the Andaman Sea. We access each through a lens that most visitors never find.

Best Time to Visit

November – February (Cool Season)

Optimal temperatures (25–32°C), low humidity, and the dry season across most regions. Chiang Mai Lantern Festival occurs in November — the country's most spectacular annual event.

Duration

10 – 16 days

Bangkok (3 days), Chiang Mai (3 days), ancient temples (2 days), Andaman islands (4 days). A complete Thailand circuit.

Experience Level

All levels

From gentle temple walks to jungle trekking, rock climbing on limestone karst, and sea kayaking. We calibrate to your preference.

Entry Requirements

30-day visa-exempt for most Western passports

US, EU, and UK passports enter visa-free. Thailand Visa on Arrival available for other nationalities. We manage all temple permits, national park fees, and private access bookings.

What We Include

Thailand
Highlights

🌅

Wat Pho & Grand Palace at Dawn

Bangkok's Royal Grand Palace complex and the Temple of the Reclining Buddha — accessed at 8 AM with a private monk-guide before the tour groups arrive.

🏔️

Chiang Mai Hill Tribe Trek

A 3-day private trek to Karen and Akha hill tribe villages with an ethnologist guide — an anthropological access that standard tours cannot replicate.

🚂

Eastern & Oriental Express

The luxury train from Bangkok to Chiang Mai — private cabin, white-tablecloth dining, and Thailand's countryside unfolding through floor-to-ceiling windows at 80km/h.

🛶

Mekong River Journey

A private longtail boat through the Golden Triangle — the confluence of Thailand, Laos, and Myanmar — and upstream to opium-history sites accessible only by water.

🏝️

Andaman Sea Private Charter

A private sailboat or catamaran through the Krabi limestone karst clusters and the Mergui Archipelago — islands that see fewer than 200 visitors per year.

🍜

Thai Culinary Immersion

A Bangkok chef's market walk, a Chiang Mai cooking class at a working farm, and a royal Thai cuisine tasting with a culinary historian — food as cultural archaeology.

Sample Journey

12 Days in Thailand.

01

Bangkok Arrival

Private transfer to a riverside luxury hotel in Bangkok. Evening Chao Phraya dinner cruise and orientation walk through the old royal quarter.

02

Bangkok Temples & Markets

Dawn visit to Wat Pho and the Grand Palace with a private monk-guide. Afternoon floating market tour. Evening Patpong cultural walk with a historian.

03

Eastern & Oriental Express Departure

Board the Eastern & Oriental Express at Bangkok Hua Lamphong for the overnight journey to Chiang Mai. Private cabin, cocktail hour in the observation car.

04–06

Chiang Mai & Hill Tribes

Chiang Mai arrival. Day 5–6: 2-day private trek to Karen hill tribe villages with an ethnologist guide. Overnight in a village homestay, no generators, no cell signal.

07

Ancient Capitals — Sukhothai

Private vehicle to Sukhothai Historical Park — the UNESCO site of Thailand's first capital. Pre-dawn access to the ruins in morning mist. Private guide with 20 years of excavation experience.

08

Ayutthaya

Thailand's second capital, destroyed by the Burmese in 1767. A city of headless Buddha statues and temple ruins reclaimed by fig trees. Private boat tour of the island city.

09–12

Andaman Sea

Domestic flight to Krabi. Board your private catamaran. 4 days sailing the Andaman Sea — Phi Phi islands, Railay Beach (accessible only by boat), and the Mergui Archipelago if tides permit.
