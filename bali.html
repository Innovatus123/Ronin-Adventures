Destination Overview

The Island
the Gods Built.

Bali has been discovered many times over — but the version most visitors see is a narrow strip of beach clubs and traffic. The real island is found at 4 AM at a temple ceremony in a village that doesn't appear in any guidebook, or on a private trekking route through the caldera of Mount Batur before the tour groups arrive.

Ronin Adventures designs Bali itineraries around access: private compound stays in Ubud with resident priests conducting morning offerings, surf coaching on uncrowded west-coast breaks, and helicopter circuits over the volcanic highlands. This is the island that earned its designation as a living cultural landscape.

Best Time to Visit

April – October (Dry Season)

Low humidity, no rain, and the temple ceremony calendar is most active. Nyepi (Day of Silence) in March/April is the island's most extraordinary cultural experience if you plan around it.

Duration

7 – 14 days

North Bali (Munduk, Singaraja) in 2 days, Ubud cultural core in 3 days, Nusa Penida island in 2 days, and coast time at your preference.

Experience Level

All levels

From gentle temple walks to serious surfing or volcano treks at 3 AM. We calibrate the itinerary to your fitness and interest profile.

Entry Requirements

Visa on Arrival

Most Western passports receive a 30-day visa on arrival, extendable once. We coordinate all logistics including temple dress, cultural briefings, and local guides.

What We Include

Bali
Highlights

🏯

Private Temple Access

Pura Lempuyang at dawn with a local priest, no crowds — the famous "Gate of Heaven" shot requires a 3:30 AM departure. We arrange it.

🌾

Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Private dawn walk through the Tegalalang irrigation system before the tour buses arrive. Breakfast with a local farming family in the terraces.

🌋

Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

3 AM departure, 2-hour ascent of an active volcano, sunrise over the Bali Sea and neighboring Mount Agung. Private guide, private permit.

🏄

West Coast Surf Access

Canggu and Medewi breaks with private surf coaching. We arrange board shaping sessions, reef safety briefings, and access to locals-only breaks.

🧘

Private Compound Retreat

Traditional Balinese compound with a resident healer, morning meditation and offering ceremonies, and a private chef using produce from the compound garden.

🐠

Nusa Penida Diving

Mola mola (sunfish) season runs July–October at Crystal Bay. Private speedboat to the island, freediving or scuba, and Kelingking Beach for dramatic coastal cliffs.

Sample Journey

10 Days in Bali.

01

Arrival in Ubud

Private transfer to a traditional compound in the Ubud rice fields. Evening temple blessing ceremony arranged with a local priest.

02

Ubud Cultural Immersion

Morning visit to Tirta Empul holy spring before crowds arrive. Afternoon batik and woodcarving workshops with master artisans. Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu at dusk.

03

Rice Terraces & Highlands

Pre-dawn walk through Tegalalang with a local farmer guide. Drive to the volcanic highlands of Kintamani for crater views. Coffee plantation tour — including the world's most controversial coffee.

04

Mount Batur Volcano Trek

3 AM departure. Two-hour trek to the active volcano summit for sunrise over three seas. Eggs cooked in volcanic steam for breakfast. Return by 10 AM.

05–06

North Bali & Munduk

Drive to the cool highlands of Munduk — waterfalls, spice plantations, and traditional longhouses. Two nights at a private clifftop villa overlooking Lake Tamblingan.

07–08

Nusa Penida Island

Private speedboat transfer. Two days of diving, snorkeling with manta rays at Manta Point, and exploration of the island's dramatic cliffside temples.

09–10

Seminyak & Departure

Two nights at a private villa in Seminyak — beach club access, sunset cocktails, rooftop dining. Private transfer to Ngurah Rai International for onward connections.
