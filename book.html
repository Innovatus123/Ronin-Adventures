What Happens Next

Four Steps
to Departure.

Once you submit your booking request, our team takes it from there. You'll never deal with logistics — only decisions about what you want your trip to feel like.

01
Confirmation Call

Within 24 hours we schedule a 20-minute call to lock in details — dates, preferences, any special requirements.

02
Deposit & Date Hold

A 20% deposit secures your dates and activates your dedicated travel advisor. No deposits means no date hold.

03
Custom Itinerary Delivery

Full day-by-day itinerary delivered within 7 days. Accommodations, guides, transfers, and experiences all arranged.

04
Pre-Departure Briefing

One week out, we walk through everything — packing, logistics, local contacts, emergency protocols. Then you go.
