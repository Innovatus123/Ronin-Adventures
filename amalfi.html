Destination Overview

Italy's Most
Dramatic Shore.

The Amalfi Coast stretches 50 kilometers along the southern edge of the Sorrentine Peninsula — clifftop villages in impossible colors, cerulean water below, lemons the size of your fist. It is one of the most visually arresting places on Earth, and one of the most visited. The difference is how you experience it.

Ronin Adventures books you into private villas above the tourist line, arranges private boat access before the day crowds arrive, and connects you with local families who have worked the terraced farms and fishing coves for generations. The Amalfi everyone dreams of — not the one they settle for.

Best Time to Visit

May – June / September – October

Shoulder season delivers warm water, manageable crowds, and open restaurants. July and August are peak season — we book villas well in advance.

Duration

5 – 10 days recommended

Ideal as a standalone or combined with Rome, Naples, or the Aeolian Islands for a broader Southern Italy circuit.

Base Options

Ravello / Positano / Praiano

Ravello sits above the fray — stunning views, fewer day-trippers. Positano for the iconic scene. Praiano for true seclusion and local authenticity.

Getting There

Naples International (NAP)

1.5 hours by private transfer. We arrange helicopter transfers from Naples for guests who prefer a 20-minute arrival with views en route.

Sample Journey

7 Days on
the Amalfi Coast.

01

Arrival into Naples

Private helicopter transfer to your clifftop villa in Ravello. Welcome dinner prepared by your private chef featuring hyper-local Campanian ingredients. Evening on the terrace.

02

Coastal Yacht Day

Full-day private yacht charter. Positano harbor, the Grotta dello Smeraldo, lunch at anchor near Capri's Faraglioni stacks. Return to villa at sunset.

03

Amalfi Town & Lemon Farms

Private guide through Amalfi's historic streets before crowds. Duomo visit with a local art historian. Afternoon at a working lemon terrace for private tasting and limoncello session.

04

Capri & Blue Grotto

Arrive at the Blue Grotto at 7am before public opening. Full island day with private driver — Villa Jovis, artisan lunch in Anacapri, sunset aperitivo at a clifftop bar.

05

Ravello Cultural Day

After-hours access to Villa Cimbrone gardens. Private concert in the Belvedere of Infinity if Ravello Festival is running. Michelin dinner reservation at Don Alfonso 1890.

06–07

Leisure & Departure

Optional morning on the Sentiero degli Dei hiking trail or time at leisure at the villa. Private transfer to Naples for onward travel or extension to Rome or Sicily.
