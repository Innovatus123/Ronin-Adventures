Destination Overview

5% of All Life
on Earth.

Costa Rica covers 0.03% of the Earth's surface and contains 5% of all species on the planet. That statistic only makes sense when you're standing in the Osa Peninsula rainforest at dawn — scarlet macaws overhead, tapir tracks in the mud, and a howler monkey alarm that carries for three kilometers.

Ronin Adventures partners with private biological reserves — not national parks — for deeper access. Private lodges in the Osa Peninsula, night hikes led by herpetologists, and surf camps on uncrowded Pacific breaks that require local knowledge to reach. Costa Rica without the zip-line tour crowds.

Best Time to Visit

December – April (Dry Season)

Pacific coast is driest November–April. Caribbean coast has a different pattern — drier June–September. The green season (May–November) has fewer tourists and lower prices.

Duration

7 – 12 days

Arenal volcano area (2 days), Monteverde cloud forest (2 days), Osa Peninsula rainforest (3 days), Pacific coast surf (2 days). A complete Costa Rica circuit.

Experience Level

All levels — very wide range

Gentle wildlife walks to class V river rafting. We calibrate the itinerary to your fitness level and interest profile.

Entry Requirements

No visa required for most Western passports

US, EU, and UK passports enter visa-free for 90 days. Yellow fever vaccination required if arriving from certain countries. We coordinate all permits and transfers.

What We Include

Costa Rica
Highlights

🌋

Arenal Volcano Access

An active stratovolcano rising 1,670m above a rainforest lake. Private naturalist hike through the 1968 lava field, hot spring soak, and nighttime lava glow viewing (when active).

☁️

Monteverde Cloud Forest

The forest that saved cloud forest conservation globally. Night walk with a herpetologist — glass frogs, red-eyed tree frogs, and fer-de-lance in a habitat that exists nowhere else.

🦁

Osa Peninsula Wildlife

The most biodiverse spot in the Western Hemisphere. Corcovado National Park by private boat, with a biologist guide who has recorded species in this reserve for 20 years.

🏄

Pacific Surf — Pavones

Pavones has one of the longest left-hand surf breaks in the world — a 1km wave on a good swell. Private coaching, uncrowded lineup, and a surf camp accessible only by dirt road.

🐊

Río Tárcoles Crocodile Safari

Private boat through a river with the highest density of American crocodiles on Earth — specimens reaching 4+ meters, visible from the boat at 3-meter distance.

☀️

Private Reserve Overnights

Lodges inside private biological reserves — accessible only on foot or by boat — where guest numbers are capped at 6 to preserve the wildlife behavior patterns.

Sample Journey

10 Days in Costa Rica.

01

San José Arrival

Private transfer to Arenal. 4-hour drive through coffee highlands. Evening arrival, volcano orientation briefing, and hot spring soak under the stars.

02

Arenal Volcano

Guided hike through the 1968 lava flow — a landscape of 50-year-old jungle reclaiming the rock. Afternoon kayak on Lake Arenal. Night hike in the surrounding forest.

03

Monteverde Cloud Forest

Drive to Monteverde (2,000m elevation). Afternoon canopy walk through the cloud forest — literally inside clouds. Night walk with a herpetologist for nocturnal species.

04

Manuel Antonio

Pacific coast drive. Afternoon at Manuel Antonio National Park — white-sand beach adjacent to dense rainforest, with capuchin monkeys visible from your beach chair.

05–06

Osa Peninsula

Domestic flight to Drake Bay. Private boat to a lodge inside the Corcovado buffer zone. Two full days with a resident biologist — morning jungle walks, afternoon mangrove kayak.

07–08

Pavones Surf Camp

Private vehicle south to Pavones. Two days of surf coaching on one of the world's longest lefts. Uncrowded water, fresh seafood at a beachside camp.

09

Río Tárcoles & Return

Morning crocodile safari by private boat. Return to San José via private vehicle. Farewell dinner with a Costa Rican cooking class — casado and tres leches.

10

Departure

Transfer to Juan Santamaría International Airport.
