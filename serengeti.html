Destination Overview

The Edge
of the Wild.

The Serengeti is not a destination — it is a force of nature. Stretching 12,000 square miles across northern Tanzania, it hosts the largest terrestrial mammal migration on Earth: over 1.5 million wildebeest, 200,000 zebra, and 500,000 gazelle moving in an unbroken cycle across the plains.

Ronin Adventures positions you at the center of it — not on a bus with forty strangers, but in private mobile camps that move with the migration, led by naturalists who have tracked these herds for decades. Every sunrise is deliberate. Every sighting, anticipated.

Best Time to Visit

July – October (Dry Season)

Peak river crossings at the Mara River. December – March for calving season in the south — a spectacle rivaling the migration itself.

Duration

7 – 14 days recommended

Minimum 5 nights to meaningfully cover migration zones. We recommend combining with Ngorongoro Crater or the Zanzibar Coast.

Experience Level

All levels — fully guided

No prior safari experience needed. All activities are led by elite naturalist guides with 4x4 vehicles equipped for any terrain.

Entry Requirements

Visa on arrival / e-Visa

Yellow fever vaccination required if arriving from endemic countries. We handle all logistics and pre-travel documentation briefings.

What We Include

Serengeti
Highlights

🌅

Private Mobile Camps

We position luxury tented camps directly in the path of the migration, moving as the herds move. No fixed lodges, no predictable views — just pure wilderness access.

🦁

Big Five Tracking

Lion prides, leopards, elephants, buffalo, and rhino. Our guides know the territory intimately — where the cats sleep at dawn, which kopjes the leopards favor.

🌊

Mara River Crossings

Witness thousands of wildebeest plunge into crocodile-filled waters in one of nature's most dramatic spectacles. We position you at precisely the right crossing point.

🎈

Hot Air Balloon Safari

Drift above the endless plains at sunrise, watching herds move below. Followed by a champagne bush breakfast — the most civilized way to see the Serengeti.

🍽️

Bush Dining Experiences

Candlelit dinners under stars, sundowner cocktails at the rim of a kopje, breakfast with a pride of lions in the distance. Every meal is an event.

🦒

Ngorongoro Extension

The world's largest intact volcanic caldera — 260 square kilometers of concentrated wildlife. Easily combined with the Serengeti for a complete northern Tanzania circuit.

Sample Journey

10 Days
in the Serengeti.

01

Arrival in Arusha

Private transfer to a luxury lodge in Arusha. Pre-safari briefing with your personal guide, gear check, and welcome dinner with views of Mount Kilimanjaro.

02

Fly into the Serengeti

Charter flight to a private airstrip deep in the Southern Serengeti. Afternoon game drive immediately upon landing — lions are often spotted within the first hour.

03–05

Migration Tracking

Full-day game drives following the herd movements. Pre-dawn departures, midday breaks at camp, golden-hour afternoon drives. Night game drives where permitted.

06

Sunrise Balloon Safari

1.5-hour balloon flight over the plains at first light. Champagne bush breakfast on landing. Afternoon at leisure or optional walking safari with armed ranger.

07–09

Ngorongoro Crater

Transfer to the Crater Highlands. Full crater descent with private vehicle — black rhino, flamingo flocks, and dense lion concentrations in a 2,000-foot volcanic bowl.

10

Zanzibar Transfer or Departure

Charter flight to Zanzibar for a 3-night beach extension, or return to Arusha/Kilimanjaro International for onward travel. Full trip documentation and custom photo book included.
